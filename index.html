Eric Zitong Zhou (周子童)

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at UCLA advised by Professor Miryung Kim. My research interests broadly span Software Engineering, Programming Languages, Compilers, and Machine Learning. Right now, I'm working on fuzzing compilers with the help of new machine learning technologies. Prior to UCLA, I did my master's at the University of Illinois (Go Illini!), working with Prof. Sasa Misailovic on the analysis of probabilistic programs.

Feel free to shoot me an email at \(\text{zitongzhou}\ at\ \text{cs.ucla.edu}\), or check out Github. Here is my CV (last updated Jan, 2026).
